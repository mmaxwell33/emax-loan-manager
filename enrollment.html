🔒 Privacy Notice & Data Consent

Before you fill this form, please read how E-EMAX Enterprise uses your personal information.

What information we collect:

  • Your full name, phone number, date of birth and home address
  • Your Ghana Card number or Passport number (for identity verification)
  • A photo of your ID document (Ghana Card / Passport)
  • Your passport-size photograph
  • Details of your guarantor and witness
  • Your loan request amount and repayment period

How we use your information:

  • To verify your identity before approving any loan
  • To process and manage your loan account
  • To contact you about repayments and your account
  • To contact your guarantor if necessary

Your rights: Your information is stored securely and is only accessible to E-EMAX Enterprise staff. We do not share your data with third parties. You may request to see or delete your data at any time by contacting E-EMAX Enterprise directly.
