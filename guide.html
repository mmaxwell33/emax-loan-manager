Section 1
What the app does

E-Max Loan Manager replaces all the paper notebooks and manual calculations. It tracks every loan from the moment it is issued until it is fully repaid — and alerts you when payments are due or late.

💳
Track every loan
Create loans with borrower details, guarantor, witness, amount, duration, and photos. All stored safely online.
📐
Auto calculation
You enter the amount and months. The app instantly calculates processing fee, monthly interest, total repayable, and monthly payment — no pen and paper needed.
✅
Record payments
Tap a button to record each monthly payment. The app tracks how much has been collected and what is still owed.
⚠️
Overdue alerts
Loans that pass their due date automatically show as Overdue in red. You can see exactly how many days late and add the penalty.
📷
Scan old documents
Take a photo of a paper loan document. The app tries to read it automatically. You review and correct, then save it digitally.
📊
Analytics & reports
See charts of monthly collections, interest earned, loan status breakdown, and your top borrowers — updated in real time.
🔗
Online applications
Send a link to new borrowers. They fill in their own details on their phone. You review and approve — no typing needed on your end.
🌍
Works everywhere
Use it on your phone, tablet, or computer. Works in Ghana and Canada. No App Store needed — installs directly from the browser.

Section 3
How to create a new loan
💡 This is the most important thing in the app. When someone comes to take a loan, follow these steps.
1

Tap "➕ New Loan" on the Dashboard or Loans screen

This opens the 5-step loan wizard. You can go back at any step if you make a mistake.

2

Step 1 — Borrower Details

Enter the borrower's full name, phone number, home address, and Ghana Card number. You can also take a photo of their face and their Ghana Card using the camera buttons. Name, phone, and address are required.

3

Step 2 — Guarantor Details

Enter the guarantor's name and phone number. The guarantor is the person who agrees to repay if the borrower defaults. Select their relationship (spouse, parent, friend, etc.).

4

Step 3 — Witness Details

Enter the witness's name and phone number. The witness is someone who saw the agreement being made.

5

Step 4 — Loan Amount & Duration

Tap one of the quick-select buttons (500, 1,000, 2,000…) or type the exact amount. Then tap or select the number of months. The loan breakdown appears automatically — it shows the processing fee, monthly payment, and total repayable. You can also add notes here.

6

Step 5 — Review & Confirm

Check all details carefully. If anything is wrong, tap "← Back". When everything is correct, tap "✅ Issue Loan". The loan is saved and appears in the Loans list.

Section 4
How to record a payment
1

Go to Loans and tap the borrower's name

This opens the loan detail screen showing the current balance, progress, and payment history.

2

Find "💳 Record Payment" section

The monthly payment amount is pre-filled. Change it only if the borrower paid a different amount.

3

If payment is late — tick "Late payment"

A 10% penalty is added automatically. You can see the penalty amount before confirming.

4

Tap "✅ Record Payment"

The payment is saved. The progress bar updates. If this was the final payment, the loan is automatically marked as Completed 🎉

Section 5
How to scan old paper documents
📚 Your mum has a stack of old paper loan records. This feature lets her take a photo and digitise them one by one.
1

Tap "📷 Scan Doc" in the menu

Two buttons appear: "Take Photo" (opens the camera) and "From Gallery" (choose an existing photo).

2

Take a clear photo of the paper document

Hold the phone steady. Make sure all the writing is visible and the paper is flat. Good lighting helps a lot.

3

Wait 10–15 seconds while the app reads the text

The app uses OCR (optical character recognition) to try to read the name, phone number, amount, and duration from the document.

4

Review and correct the extracted data

The app fills in what it could read. Always check every field — OCR can make mistakes, especially with handwriting. Fix any errors by tapping on the field and typing the correct value.

5

Tap "✅ Create Loan from This Document"

The loan is saved digitally. The photo is also stored so you have a record of the original paper.

💡 Tip: If the app can't read the handwriting, that's okay — just type the details manually. The important thing is to save the photo and the information together.

Section 9
How the loan math works

The app does all calculations automatically, but here is how it works so you can verify.

The three charges
💸
Processing Fee — 4%
Paid BEFORE the borrower receives the money. GH₵ 40 for every GH₵ 1,000 borrowed.
📅
Interest — 10% per month
Applied to the full principal for every month of the loan. Not reducing balance.
⚠️
Late Penalty — 10% of monthly payment
Added if a payment is not made on the due date. Example: if monthly payment is GH₵ 350, the penalty is GH₵ 35.
🧮 Example: GH₵ 1,000 for 4 months
Principal (amount taken)GH₵ 1,000
Processing fee (4% × 1,000)−GH₵ 40
Amount received by borrowerGH₵ 960
Interest / month (10% × 1,000)GH₵ 100
Total interest (100 × 4 months)GH₵ 400
Total to repay (1,000 + 400)GH₵ 1,400
Monthly payment (1,400 ÷ 4)GH₵ 350
Late penalty if missed (10% × 350)+GH₵ 35

Free vs Paid — Supabase & Vercel

The app uses two free services. Here is what the free tiers include and when you would need to upgrade.

🗄️ Supabase (database)
FeatureFreePro ($25/mo)
Database storage500 MB8 GB
Rows of dataUnlimitedUnlimited
File storage (photos)1 GB100 GB
Project pausingPauses after 1 week inactiveNever pauses
BackupsDaily (7 days)Daily (30 days)
⚠️ Important: The free tier pauses if no one logs in for 7 days. When it resumes (takes ~30 seconds) all data is safe. Upgrade to Pro to avoid this.

For a loan business with 50–500 active loans and photo storage, 500 MB is more than enough for 1–2 years. Upgrade when you reach ~400 MB used.

🚀 Vercel (hosting)

DeploymentsUnlimitedUnlimited

HTTPS✅ Auto✅ Auto

✅ The free Vercel Hobby plan is more than enough for this app forever. You only need Pro if you have a business team managing deployments together.

With a small loan business in Ghana, you will never come close to the 100 GB/month bandwidth limit.

💡 My recommendation

Start on the free tier of both — it costs nothing. If your mum is using it daily and data is growing, upgrade Supabase to Pro ($25/month ≈ GH₵ 280/month at current rates) to avoid the sleep issue. Vercel free is fine forever for this use case.

The easiest way to avoid the sleep issue for free: have someone log in at least once per week. Since your sister in Canada can log in from there, the project will stay awake.

Section 11
Security & Data Safety
🔐
Password protection
Only people with an account can see loan data. Supabase handles the login — passwords are never stored as plain text.
🛡️
Row-Level Security
Even if someone guessed your API key, they still cannot read your data. The database enforces that each user only sees their own records.
🔒
HTTPS everywhere
All data is encrypted in transit. Vercel provides HTTPS automatically — the padlock icon always shows in the browser.
☁️
Cloud backup
Data is stored in Supabase's cloud (hosted in Ireland). Daily automatic backups. Even if the phone is lost, all data is safe.
🚨 Never share your password with anyone. If you think your account has been accessed by someone else, go to Settings → Account → Sign Out on all devices, then reset your password via the login screen.
